This is an angle quiz that quizzes you on Compelentary angles(Adds to 90 degrees) and Supplementary Angles (Adds to 180 degrees) For both, you must subtract y to find the other angle which is X (The answer).

If you wonder whats complementary, it is when the first line and the last line of the angle looks like a corner of a square so you should subtract y with 90 degrees to find x which is the answer as supplementary is when the first and last lines join eachother to look like one line on top of the dividing one, in this case subtract y from 180 degrees to find x which is the answer.
